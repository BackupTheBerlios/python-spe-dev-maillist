Check out latest version, mainly this is new:

* mac fixes
Got rid of a lot of segfaults, please report remaining ones.Having a mac
myself, they are now much more easy to solve.

* workspaces
Probably in the future SPE will have a full featured project manager, but
for now Thurston proposed to introduce 'workspaces'. A workspace is a
collection of all open files, recent files, folders of the path browser and
notes. They can be opened and saved in SPE. This workspace could then also
store breakpoints, the main script and debug command line options.
Warning: This still needs some work! The best is now if you work with SPE to
immediately save your workspace as something and turn on remember files
(heart icon on toolbar) otherwise you loose your recent files etc... This
will be fixed soon.

* smart indent
SPE now dedents automatically after break, return, etc...

Special thanks to Thurston Stone for his contribution!

News: SPE on PyCon
It has been brought to my attention that SPE will be presented on PyCon as
one of the four most promising IDE's:
http://wiki.python.org/moin/PyCon2006/Talks#19
Anyone who thinks having advice how SPE could perform good in this review,
can come up with ideas. Especially people who want to add it to the code.
This is btw why I put smart indentation in SPE as it was very easy.
